These terms outline the booking, rescheduling, and cancellation policies at Bala Saraswathi Conventional Hall. We strive to make your experience convenient, flexible, and transparent.

1. Booking Process

  • Bookings can be made online, by phone, or directly at the venue.
  • A minimum advance amount (as specified by the management) is required to reserve the hall.
  • The remaining balance must be paid before or on the day of the event.
  • Booking confirmation will be provided via email or SMS once the payment is received.

2. Rescheduling Policy

  • Date changes are allowed subject to availability and management approval.
  • If the new date is unavailable, cancellation rules will apply.

3. Cancellation Policy

  • More than 30 days before the event: 100% refund of advance (excluding service charges).
  • 15 to 29 days before the event: 50% refund of advance.
  • Less than 15 days before the event: No refund.
  • No-show: No refund will be given.
