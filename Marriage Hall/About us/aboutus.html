About us

A Traditional Kalyana Mandapam

Bala Saraswathi Kalyana Mandapam is one of the most trusted and fortunate conventional halls in Thoothukudi, designed to make every special occasion truly memorable. With spacious interiors, modern facilities, and a touch of tradition, our mandapam is ideal for weddings, receptions, family gatherings, and cultural events. Since 2022, we have been known for providing a warm and welcoming atmosphere, combining cultural values with modern convenience to ensure celebrations are smooth and stress-free. Families choose Bala Saraswathi Kalyana Mandapam not just for a venue, but for a place filled with positive energy, blessings, and memories that last a lifetime.
